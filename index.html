About Us

KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology

Established in 2009, KPRIET is a premier engineering and technology institute known for innovative teaching, cutting-edge research, and strong industry collaborations. The institute fosters world-class education, driving innovation, entrepreneurship, and industrial consultancy. With a highly skilled faculty, talented students, and efficient administration, KPRIET excels in academic performance, sustainability initiatives, and social responsibility, making a significant impact on knowledge mobilization and real-world problem-solving.

Department of Mechatronics

The Department of Mechatronics Engineering at KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology, established in 2022, offers a B.E. program focusing on robotics, automation, control systems, and sensors. Students gain theoretical knowledge and practical experience through labs, projects, and industry internships. The Department emphasizes research in autonomous systems, and mechatronics product design, supported by expert faculty and state - of - art facilities

Chakravyuha

Chakravyuha is a National Level Technical Fest organized by the MI QUINIX Mechatronics Association in collaboration with IEEE RAS and IEEE VTS. Inspired by the legendary battle formation of the Mahabharata, it symbolizes strategy, innovation, and resilience. The fest brings together students from across the nation to compete and collaborate in robotics, electric vehicles, automation, drones, coding, and next-gen technologies, creating a dynamic platform for learning and innovation.
